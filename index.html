Research Participation Agreement

This study pilots a teacher-curated maritime ESP bridge simulator designed through teacher-led vibe coding. It investigates how genre-based and CLIL-informed tasks can support professional maritime writing.

Collected data: student identifier, consent status, questionnaires, simulator interactions, decisions, selected evidence, written drafts, optional external AI revision logs and timestamps. Data should be anonymised in any publication.
